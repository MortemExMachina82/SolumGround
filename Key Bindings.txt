Key Bindings:
    • Move Forward Press W
    • Move Left Press A
    • Move Backward Press S
    • Move Right Press D
    
    • Jump Press Space
    • Sprint Press Left Shift
    
    • Turn Left Press Left Arrow Key
    • Turn Right Press Right Arrow Key
    • Turn Up Press Up Arrow Key
    • Turn Down Press Down Arrow Key
    
    • Turn Move Mouse
    • Place Block Press Right Mouse Button
    • Break Block Press Left Mouse Button
    
    • Change Hotbar Block To The Left Press O
    • Change Hotbar Block To The Right Press P
    
    • Toggle Fullscreen Press ALT And F
    • Toggle Show Sky Box Press ALT And S
    
    • ReSpawn Press ALT+R
    
    • Increse Render Distance Press ALT And +
    • Decrese Render Distance Press CTRL And -
    
    • Quit And Save Press Escape
